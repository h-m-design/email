Workspace
On the Workspace homepage you can see the projects you're a member of and view the other members. Invite new members with just a couple of clicks, giving them access to all the projects in that Workspace.
Get Started
Global search
Search across your entire account, your projects, and your Workspace by typing the title of an asset, folder, or project. Within a project, use the search field to find assets and folders. Searches can also be saved as Collections.
Learn More
Enhanced notifications
More granular notification settings let you fine tune the types of notifications you want to receive and how you get them. We've introduced independent controls for email and iOS push, along with each notification type.
Take Control
Faster, more reliable uploads
The new upload sheet lets you monitor all your uploads across your entire account from anywhere in the app. Prioritize uploads with the pause button, and easily access your upload history.
