We’re proud to be hosting our very first
Coffee with Pride morning — and you’re invited.
 
In celebration of Global Pride Month and in partnership with Pride Pledge, we’re welcoming the Gaybourhood to our Freemans Bay space to celebrate and connect with our Rainbow community.
 
Here’s what to expect:
  • Fresh brews of our latest single origin, Brazil Fazenda Dona Nenem
  • A quick intro from Allpress on why Pride matters to us — and our commitment to a safe, inclusive workplace for all
  • A welcome from Martin King, Director of Pride Pledge, who’ll share more about our partnership and the power of the Gaybourhood.

It's all happening this Wednesday 25 June at 8:30am.
